About

Amanda works in stoneware clay making pots inspired by the rural fenland countryside where she lives and many visits to the Norfolk Coast. The pottery is fired at a high temperature giving it a warm weathered appearance.

Seven years ago she stumbled on the Unique Cottage Studio and decided to try a few classes. Amanda now has a Studio in her Lincolnshire garden.
